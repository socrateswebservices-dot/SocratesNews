The University of Ilorin (UNILORIN) has officially announced the postponement of its 2025/2026 academic session matriculation ceremony.

The event, which was initially scheduled to hold on Tuesday, November 18, 2025, will no longer take place on that date.

University management has confirmed that a new date has yet to be finalized. The institution will communicate the revised schedule to the new students and the general public as soon as an official decision is reached.

Newly admitted students are advised to monitor the university's official website and communication channels for the forthcoming announcement regarding the new date. Meanwhile, academic activities, including lectures and registration, are expected to continue as scheduled.

NOTE: This article is based on information provided by the university regarding the postponement. Students should rely only on official UNILORIN sources for the confirmed new date.
