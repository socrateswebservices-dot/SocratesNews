ADEKUNLE AJASIN UNIVERSITY STUDENTS’ UNION

FROM: OFFICE OF THE PUBLIC RELATIONS OFFICER

TO: THE PUBLIC. 

This is to inform the general public most especially candidates that sat for 2025 UTME and scored 160 and above that Adekunle Ajasin University, Akungba Akoko Post UTME Screening portal has been reopened for for fresh application.  The registration will be closed by midnight today 17th of November 2025.

Interested candidates are advised to change their first choice of institution on JAMB portal to AAUA and proceed to the application portal via this link  https://eduportal.aaua.edu.ng/aug/applications to apply for the available courses. 

Candidates who are willing to seize the opportunity and be admitted are advised to first all check the requirements of their choice of course and make sure these requirements are met in order to be considered for admission.
