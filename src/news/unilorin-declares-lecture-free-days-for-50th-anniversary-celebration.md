The University of Ilorin (UNILORIN) has officially announced several days as lecture-free to allow students and staff to participate fully in the celebration of the university's 50th anniversary.

The management of the institution has declared the following days as lecture-free:

* **Monday, 24th November**
* **Wednesday, 26th November**
* **Thursday, 27th November**

The measure is effective immediately and is designed to ensure maximum participation in the golden jubilee events marking five decades since the university's founding. Students and faculty are advised to note these changes in their academic schedules.
