NUC grants UCH approval to offer Bachelor of Nursing Science degree

 The National Universities Commission (NUC) has granted approval to the School of Nursing, University College Hospital (UCH), Ibadan, to commence a full-time Bachelor of Nursing Science (B.NSc) degree programme in affiliation with the University of Ibadan (UI). The approval was officially conveyed in a letter dated November 6, 2025, marking a major milestone in the hospital’s academic development .
